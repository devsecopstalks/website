This time [Johan Abildskov](https://twitter.com/randomsort), a Senior Consultant with [Praqma/Eficode](https://www.praqma.com), joins us to talk about SemVer (Semantic Versioning), and we finally get to hear what Julien has to say about it. We get to explore different options regarding versioning and how it helps humans communicate. At the end of the podcast, everyone gets to share their approach and recommendations for versioning things.
