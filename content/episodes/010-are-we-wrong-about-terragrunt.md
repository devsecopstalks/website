Our guest speaker is [Anton Babenko](https://www.linkedin.com/in/antonbabenko/), he is a DevSecOps Talks podcast fan, AWS Community Hero, Terraform fanatic, HashiCorp Ambassador and a prolific open source contributor. After listening to episode [#9 Terraform in CI](https://devsecops.fm/episodes/009-terraform-in-ci/) and [#1 Infrastructure as Code](https://devsecops.fm/episodes/001-infrastructure-as-code/), Anton decided that enough is enough and volunteered to give his point of view on Terragrunt since he thought that we are missing a few important points. In this episode, we are discussing the use cases of [Terragrunt](https://terragrunt.gruntwork.io/), a wrapper around Terraform for working with multiple environments and modules.
